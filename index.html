Afang Soup

An Efik/Ibibio Delicacy made primarily with Afang leaves and waterleaf

Prep Time: 40 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Servings: 5 Servings

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • afang leaf
  • water leaf
  • palm oil
  • crayfish
  • goat meat or beef
  • salt,onion and seasoned cubes
  • ground fresh pepper
  • periwinkle and stockfish

Instructions

  1. wash and season your goat meat, then cook till tender
  2. Add palm oil to the pot and cook for 5 minutes
  3. Add crayfish, pepper, onion, seasoning cubes, and salt. Stir well
  4. Add the chopped waterleaf and allow it to release moisture.
  5. Gently fold in the Afang leaves and simmer for 10 minutes
  6. Adjust seasoning and serve hot with garri, fufu, or pounded yam.

Chef's Tips

Note: Do not add too much to you cooking as the water leaf already contains lots of water

Also, wash the waterleaf so well so as not to purge
